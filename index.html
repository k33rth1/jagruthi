About Our College

Sponsored by Jagruti Education and Welfare Society, Jagruti Institute of Engineering and Technology (JIET) principal goal is student focus. This means that we will drive our practices and decisions by the needs of students. In doing this, JIET uses holistic approach.

The college provides its students with academic, extracurricular and other resources that help them achieve highest scholarly levels and prepare them for positions of leadership and lives of service in many fields of human endeavor.
